Vector convention shown here is a simplified frontal-plane teaching vector: atrial depolarization is usually inferior/leftward; ventricular depolarization is dominated by left ventricular mass and travels generally inferior-leftward before terminal superior/rightward forces. Pre-excitation, ventricular rhythms, and fibrillation distort or abolish that organized loop.
